    <h1 style="margin-top: 0; font-size: 1.3em;">bio</h1>
    
    <p>Rebecca Zhang (b. 2004) is a multidisciplinary maker from New York City working in paper and clay, with a particular interest in book arts and teaware. They are currently completing their BFA at the New York State College of Ceramics at Alfred University, where they studied ceramics and graphic design.</p>
    
    <p>In New York City, Rebecca is an instructor at Choplet Ceramics Studio and has led workshops at SAGE, an LGBTQ+ senior center. They have exhibited work and completed a month-long residency at JCU in Jingdezhen, China. Rebecca was nominated and selected as one of the 2026 Windgate-Lamar Fellows by the Center of Craft. This summer, Rebecca will join Anderson Ranch Arts Center as a ceramics intern.</p>
